# David's Workout Program

## Overview

| Weeks | Phase | Style | Days/Week |
|-------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1–3 | **Phase A** — Slow TUL Training | HIT-based, 5s up / 5s down, 1 set to fatigue | 2 (Mon, Thu) |
| 4–6 | **Phase B** — Hypertrophy Split | Controlled tempo, double progression | 4 (Mon, Wed, Thu, Sat) |
| Repeat cycle |

---

## PHASE A (Weeks 1–3)

**Tempo:** 5 sec up / 5 sec down
**Sets:** 1 per exercise (to fatigue)
**Rest:** 60–90 sec between exercises
**Goal:** 60–90 sec Time Under Load (TUL)

### Monday — Workout A (Primary Machine Circuit)

| # | Exercise | Sets | TUL |
|---|----------|------|-----|
| 1 | Seated Row (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 2 | Chest Press (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 3 | Lat Pulldown (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 4 | Shoulder Press (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 5 | Leg Press (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 6 | Leg Curl (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 7 | Butt Blaster / Glute Machine (Monday only) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 8 | Abdominal Machine | 1 | 45–75s |

**Notes:**
- Train to true muscular fatigue
- Maintain strict slow tempo
- Last 10–15 sec: partials or static hold
- When TUL > 90 sec → increase weight

### Thursday — Workout B (Variation + Add-ons)

**Main Circuit:**

| # | Exercise | Sets | TUL |
|---|----------|------|-----|
| 1 | Incline Chest Press (Machine) | 1 | 60–90s |
| 2 | Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown OR Assisted Pull-Up | 1 | 60–90s |
| 3 | Chest-Supported Row Machine | 1 | 60–90s |
| 4 | Lateral Raise Machine | 1 | 60–75s |
| 5 | Hack Squat Machine OR Split Squat Machine | 1 | 60–90s |
| 6 | Leg Extension Machine | 1 | 60–75s |
| 7 | Cable Crunch or Ab Machine | 1 | 45–75s |

**Add-On Finishers (Thursday only):**

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps |
|---|----------|------|------|
| 8 | Standing Calf Raise (Machine) | 2 | 12–20 |
| 9 | Cable Tricep Pushdown | 2 | 10–12 |
| 10 | Dumbbell Hammer Curl | 2 | 10–12 |

**Notes:**
- Same slow tempo for main circuit
- Finishers = controlled but not ultra slow
- Track each variation separately
- Add weight when top performance reached

## Progression Rules

### Double Progression (Phase B)
- Hit top of rep range → increase weight next session
- Miss target → keep weight until achieved

### TUL Progression (Phase A)
- Goal: 60–90 sec
- TUL > 90 sec → increase weight
- TUL < 60 sec → decrease weight

### Effort
- Final reps should be near failure or failure
- Maintain strict form always

### Deload (Every 6–8 Weeks)
- Reduce weight by 15–20% OR cut total sets in half
