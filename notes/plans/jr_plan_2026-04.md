## Section A: Single Conversion Path

**Enforced path — no competing CTAs:**

```
Assessment → Report → Free 15-min Call → Community / Partner Resources
```

| Step | CTA | Where It Appears |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Take assessment | "Take the Joy Assessment" | Homepage, /assessments/, emails, social, partners |
| Read report | Delivered automatically post-assessment | Email + on-screen |
| Book call | "Book a Free 15-min Call with David" | Inside report, /assessments/ page, /coaching/ page, post-assessment email |
| Join community | "Join the Joy Restart Community" | Inside report (secondary CTA), community page, post-call follow-up |
| Explore resources | Partner resources, next assessment | Inside report (tertiary), /resources/ page |

**Rule:** Every page and email has ONE primary CTA. The primary CTA is always the next step in the path, not a skip-ahead.

## Section B: Funnel Map

| Step | Action | Expected % | Current Issue | Improvement |
|------|--------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Email → Click | Open email, click assessment link | 25-35% open, 10-15% click | No emails sent yet | Strong subject lines, clear single CTA |
| Click → Start | Land on assessment, begin filling in | 70-80% | Registration form (name, email, age, gender) may cause drop-off | Keep form minimal, explain why info is needed |
| Start → Complete | Answer all questions, submit | 75-85% | 50 questions across 5 pages — some may abandon mid-way | Progress indicator visible, encouraging section transitions |
| Complete → Report Read | Receive and open report | 85-90% | ⚠️ Unknown — need to verify delivery method (email? on-screen?) | Ensure instant delivery + email backup |
| Report → Call Booked | Click CTA in report, book call | 15-25% | ⚠️ Free call CTA may be weak or missing in report | Prominent CTA at end of report, warm language |
| Call → Community/Next | Join MN or take another assessment | 40-50% | Depends on call quality (David's strength) | David recommends next step during call |

**Overall target:** 500 emails → 50-75 clicks → 35-55 start → 25-45 complete → 5-10 calls booked

## Section C: 7-Day Post-Assessment User Journey

| Day | Channel | Message | Goal |
|-----|---------|---------|------|
| Day 0 | On-screen + Email | "Your report is ready" — report delivered with free call CTA | Deliver value, offer call |
| Day 1 | Email | "What your results mean" — key insight from their assessment domain + "Ready to talk?" | Re-engage, push toward call |
| Day 3 | Email | "You're not alone" — introduce MN community, share what others are experiencing | Build belonging, secondary CTA |
| Day 5 | Email | "Go deeper" — suggest second assessment based on their first one | Increase engagement, repeat cycle |
| Day 7 | Email | "Still thinking about your results?" — final nudge for free call + curated resource | Last push for conversion |

**Rule:** Each email has ONE primary CTA. Day 0-1 push the call. Day 3+ pivot to community and deeper engagement if call wasn't booked.
