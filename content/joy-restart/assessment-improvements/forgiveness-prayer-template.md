Thank You for the forgiveness You have already worked in [First Name]'s heart — especially in [highest scoring domain].

Where [lowest scoring domain] still feels difficult or incomplete, we ask for Your healing hand.

[If lowest is Decisional Forgiveness:]

[If lowest is Emotional Forgiveness:]

"Get rid of all bitterness, rage and anger, brawling and slander, along with every form of malice. Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you" (Ephesians 4:31-32).

Lord, forgiveness is Your gift to us — and through us to others. Complete this work in [First Name]'s heart, in Your time and by Your grace.

In Jesus' name,
