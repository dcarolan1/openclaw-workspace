# Updated Consent Checkbox Text

## Current (inaccurate):
"I consent to the anonymous collection of my report data to improve this assessment, with no personal data shared."

## Problem:
We ARE collecting personal data (name + email) alongside assessment responses. The current text implies we don't collect personal data at all.

## Recommended replacement:

**Option A (concise):**
"I consent to Joy Restart collecting my responses to generate my personalized report. Anonymous, de-identified data may be used to improve our assessments. My individual results are confidential."

**Option B (more detailed):**
"I consent to Joy Restart collecting my name, email, and assessment responses to generate my personalized report. With my consent, anonymous data from my responses may be used to improve our assessments. My individual results are confidential and will not be shared. I may request deletion of my data at any time by contacting support@joyrestart.com."

## Recommendation:
Use Option A on the form. Link to the full Disclosure page for the detailed version.
