# Joy Restart — Assessment Disclaimer & Disclosure

_This page should be linked from every assessment's "Disclaimer & Disclosure" link._

---

## What This Assessment Is

Joy Restart assessments are self-reflection tools designed to help you grow in your relationship with God and others. They are grounded in Scripture, informed by research in Christian maturity, attachment, and emotional health, and developed in partnership with recognized ministry and academic organizations.

These assessments are **not** diagnostic tools. They do not provide clinical, psychological, or medical advice. Your results reflect your self-reported responses at this point in time — they are a starting point for growth, not a clinical evaluation.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional or call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).

## What We Collect

When you take an assessment, we collect:
- Your name and email address (to identify your report)
- Your age range and gender (to improve our assessments)
- Your assessment responses (to generate your personalized report)

## How Your Data Is Used

- Your responses are used to generate a **personalized report** delivered to you on screen at the time of completion.
- If you choose to email your results, your report will be sent to the email address you provide.
- With your consent, **anonymous, de-identified** data from your responses may be used in aggregate to improve our assessments. No individual responses are shared publicly or with third parties.

## Confidentiality

Your individual assessment results are **confidential**. We do not share your personal responses or report with anyone outside Joy Restart unless you choose to share them yourself.

We encourage you to share your report with a trusted friend, counselor, or pastor — discussion multiplies growth (Hebrews 10:24).

## Data Storage & Retention

Your assessment responses are stored securely on our servers. We retain your data only as long as needed to provide our services and improve our assessments. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@joyrestart.com.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Request a copy of your stored data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for anonymous data use at any time

Contact: support@joyrestart.com

## Consent

By taking this assessment, you agree that:
- You are participating voluntarily
- This is not a clinical or diagnostic tool
- Your results are for personal growth and reflection
- Joy Restart may use anonymous, de-identified aggregate data to improve assessments
